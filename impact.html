Our Impact

Since launching, we have registered 1000 job seekers, partnered with 300 employers, listed 400 opportunities, and successfully matched 600 candidates with employment opportunities. These early results demonstrate demand for our solution and validate our model. By reducing the gap between available talent and employment opportunities, we are not only helping people find work—we are enabling individuals to earn income, support their families, and contribute to economic growth. With investment, we aim to scale our reach, improve our matching technology, and connect thousands more people to meaningful employment.

So far, we have helped match over 70% of graduates with suitable employers within 3 months after graduation.
